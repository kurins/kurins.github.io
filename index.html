(EZ)Expense

(EZ)Expense is simple, easy to use mobile app to help business travelers to track and submit their expenses with single tap and get paid with no sweat!

The user

  • The user is an employee of a large organization who is traveling (or has recently traveled) and is required to submit an expense report for purchases made during the trip
  • Typically, users have a company-issued credit card that I use to charge all my travel expenses.
  • Users have a company-issued smartphone (Android or iOS device)

    • Timing

  • Reports must be submitted within 90 days of expense
  • Users have to wait for transactions to post to my credit card account in order to expense it

    • Details, details:

  • Some purchases (anything over $75, hotel stays, car rentals) will require a receipt to be submitted
  • Some purchases (meals) will require a receipt and names of attendees

    • Receipts

  • Most of the users are very disorganized and usually can’t find all the receipts in one place after the trip
  • Users may forget to get a receipt
    • Prototype: (EZ)Expense
    • Date: Dec '14 - Jan '15
    • Service: UX Design

    Problem

    In a company , we are seeing most of the employees procrastinate submitting their travel / business expenses.

    Broader issues are :

  • Timing (submitting the expense late > 90 days)
  • Missing Receipts
  • Claims information are incomplete

    • User Experience Process

    Who was interviewed?

  • 9 users in total
  • 3 Frequent Business Travellers
  • 4 non-frequent travellers
  • 2 Administrative assistants

    • Interview Results

    1. Time
      1. Have to wait for transactions to post to my credit card
    2. Process
      1. Some purchases (anything over $75, hotel stays, car rentals) will require a receipt to be submitted
      2. Some purchases (meals) will require a receipt and names of attendees
      3. Initial setup in mobile phone is cumbersome
    3. Receipts
      1. Oops , receipts are missing
      2. I forget to get a receipt
      3. Manually had to scan and send receipts

    Affinity Map










    Based on user research, I came up with Affinity map to cluster the core pain points.

    Solution





    Nobody likes to keep track of all their expenses. If app provides a quick way to capture the expense, Users probably will use it. This will make users submit the expenses on timely manners and get paid quickly.
